Circulatory System

In biology class we are learning about the systems and the functions of the human body. I decided to do the the circulatory system. Reason why i decided to do the circulatory system was because its the most important system of the human body.

For this project I did a poster and a powerpoint. I really enjoyed this project because I like learning about the human body.

What's the circulatory system?

The circulatory system is a vast network of organs and vessels that is responsible for the flow of blood, nutrients, hormones, oxygen and other gases to and from cells. Without the Circulatory system , the body would not be able to fight diseases or maintain the body stable.

My Poster

In my poster I drew a human body, so as you can se there are alot of red and blue lines on it those are arteries which are the red ones and the veins which are the blue ones.I also added the heart because the heart is also the main part of the Circulatory system.
